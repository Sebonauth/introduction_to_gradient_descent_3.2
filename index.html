The Basic Principle

At its core, gradient descent is an optimization algorithm used to find the minimum of a function. In machine learning, this function is often the loss function, which measures how well a model is performing.

Continue

Just like our blindfolded friend on the hill, we take small steps in the direction that decreases the loss the most.

Continue

Mathematically, this involves computing the gradient (or derivative) of the loss function with respect to the model parameters and updating the parameters in the opposite direction.

Build Your Vocab

Gradient

The gradient is a vector that points in the direction of the greatest rate of increase of a function. In the context of gradient descent, we use the negative gradient to find the direction of the greatest rate of decrease.
